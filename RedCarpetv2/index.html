RedCarpet

RedCarpet is built with HTML and CSS3 that you can play this game by using any Browser. This is simple quize game. In this game letters from english word or Sentence are placed at different blocks. Players have to find English letters of that word or Sentence by passing through the pointers. These Pointers help to player to find all the words across the pannel and complete the sentence.


Quast: Find all the letters, make sentence, message me the hindi meaning of that sentence. You will be rewarded with Solution of previous year EDM Question Paper.
